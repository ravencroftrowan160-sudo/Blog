About Me

Get to know the author behind the blog

Who Am I?

Welcome! My name is Rowan Ravencroft, and I'm a passionate web developer and content creator dedicated to sharing knowledge about engineering, programming, and modern technology. I'm new to the world of blogging and hope to provide valuable insights as well as share stories from my journey as a student/developer.

My Mission

The goal of this blog is to create high-quality, accessible content that helps developers at all levels improve their skills. Whether you're just starting your coding journey or looking to deepen your expertise, you'll find valuable insights and practical examples here.

What You'll Find Here

  • Web Development Tutorials: Step-by-step guides for HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks
  • Best Practices: Industry standards and proven patterns for writing better code
  • Tool Reviews: In-depth reviews of development tools and technologies
  • Career Advice: Tips for growing as a developer and advancing your career

My Journey

I started coding in 2015 with a simple curiosity about how websites work. Since then, I've worked on numerous projects ranging from small personal sites to large-scale web applications. Through this journey, I've learned that the best way to solidify knowledge is by teaching others.

Let's Connect

I'd love to hear from you! Whether you have questions, feedback, or just want to say hello, feel free to contact me. You can also follow me on social media to stay updated with the latest posts and announcements.
